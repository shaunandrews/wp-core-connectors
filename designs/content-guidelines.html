Content Guidelines

Define how content should look, read, and feel across your site. These guidelines help maintain consistency whether you're writing, designing, or generating content with AI.

Site

Describe your site's purpose, goals, and primary audience.

This helps creators develop content that resonates with your readers.

Copy

Set your writing standards for tone, voice, style, and formatting.

Include brand terminology and content to avoid so all writing stays consistent.

Images

Outline your style, dimensions, formats, mood and aesthetic preferences.

These preferences apply when generating or selecting images across your site.

Blocks

Create tailored guidelines for specific block types.

Additional guidelines

Include any other standards such as SEO or legal requirements.

Catch-all for standards that don't fit the categories above.
